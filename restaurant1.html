UB City is a business district in Bengaluru, India.
            It consists of 6 blocks with a total built up area of over 1.6 million sq ft.
            Pioneered by the UB Group in a joint venture with Prestige Group. 
            it is built on 13 acres (53,000 m2) of land and hosts 1,000,000 sq ft (93,000 m2) of high-end commercial, retail and service apartment space
